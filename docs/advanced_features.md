- **How it works:** 
  - Analyzes document content using OCR
  - Identifies document type (passport, bill, certificate, etc.)
  - Auto-assigns to appropriate category
  - Suggests tags based on content
- **Tech Stack:** Google ML Kit, TensorFlow Lite
- **User Value:** Zero manual organization needed

**Document Insights**
- Extract key information automatically:
  - Expiry dates from passports/licenses
  - Due dates from bills
  - Important numbers (account numbers, policy numbers)
  - Contact information from business cards
- **Smart Alerts:** Proactive reminders before expiry

**Document Search with NLP**
- Natural language search: "Show passport expiring this year"
- Semantic search: Understands intent, not just keywords
